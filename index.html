Marsha Blackburn, Senior Senator for Tennessee
Congress / Members of Congress / Map / Tennessee
Blackburn is the senior senator from Tennessee and is a Republican. She has served since Jan 3, 2019. Blackburn is next up for reelection in 2024 and serves until Jan 3, 2025. She was previously the representative for Tennessee’s 7th congressional district as a Republican from 2003 to 2018.
